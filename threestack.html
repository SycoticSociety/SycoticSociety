About Sycotic Society. Our History.


Born from the gaming revolution!

The thrill of victory, and the agnony of defeat.

As a young man I can clearly remember the first gaming console my father bought me. We were living in Puerto Rico at the time. I was roughly nine years old at the time. Honestly, I really couldn't believe he actually bought it for me. In those days a 8 bit system ran about $235.00. Which was basically a small fortune as far as I was concerned. The drive home took forever, or so it seemed. The anticipation was basically killing me. Once we arrived home, I dashed inside sat on the floor and started shifting through all the contents. Everything almost seemed alien to me. Needless to say, I figured it out! The Connection was complete! My television went from a old run down scrambled screen, to a technological powerhouse! I was immediately ported to another world. Suddenly, I could feel my neurons were firing rapidly. I could feel myself gettimg warmer. Real physical changes were actually taking place inside me. Little did I realize, I would never be the same. Years had passed, and there was little end in sight. I started collecting game after game. Playing arcade games at local pizza parlors, and corner stores. The techno-Boom had arrived! To Be Continued!!!
